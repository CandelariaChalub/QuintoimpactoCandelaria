MATHEMATICS

In this mathematics course you will learn a series of logical concepts associated with quantitative reasoning, to carry out the solution of different practical problems associated with daily life. It is easy to learn and only requires basic knowledge.


PHYSICS

In this physics course you will learn how the universe works and how the physical phenomena of reality influence our existence. It is easy to learn and only requires basic knowledge.


FRENCH

In this French course for beginners you will learn the basic grammatical and communicative fundamentals of the language, which will allow you to introduce yourself and the members of your family, make invitations, indicate the time or climate, and in general you will be able to establish a first contact in this language. .


PHILOSOPHY

In this philosophy course you will learn about how critical and reflective thinking has been forged, from the study of reason, based on different historical periods and philosophical schools. It is easy to learn and only requires basic knowledge.
